Ratatouille

Description

This is Gino D'acampo's recipe for a spicy rataouille variant, prepared with eggs. We have been following this recipe for years, as it is hearty, simple to prepare, and full of flavour.

It is a firm family favourite in our house, even enjoyed by our little one (who tends to usually hate anything that isn't mac n' cheese).

Ingredients

  • Red peppers
  • Yellow peppers
  • Aubergines
  • Red Onions
  • Flat-leaf parsley
  • Fine green beans
  • Chilli flakes
  • Tinned borlotti beans
  • Tinned Cannellini beans
  • Tinned chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/gas mark 4. Place all the vegetables in a large, shallow roasting tin. Drizzle over the oil and vinegar and sprinkle over the thyme, salt and pepper. Toss everything together to combine and roast for 1 hour, turning all the vegetables halfway through the roasting time.
  2. Meanwhile, make the sauce. Heat the oil in a medium saucepan over a medium heat. Add the chilli and garlic and fry for 30 seconds then pour in the passata and add the shredded basil and salt. Simmer gently for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Tip the roasted vegetables into the sauce. Check the seasoning once more and stir gently to combine.
  4. Spoon the ratatouille onto a large serving platter, garnish with the whole basil leaves, scatter over the Parmesan and drizzle over a little oil. Serve immediately.
